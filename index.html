Lead

School

Organizing the 24 Hour Wake at your school is a great way to gain leadership and fundraising experience, meet new friends and leaders (like yourself!), raise awareness about HIV/AIDS, and rack up those volunteer hours. Best of all, your work will help children around the world!

Resources: Pledge forms, permission forms, poster.

Business/Not-for-Profit

The 24 Hour Wake is a unique team-building experience and a great way to strengthen your businessâ€™ relationship with the community. Treat your employees to a stress and work free night aimed at recognizing the struggles faced by millions of HIV-affected people worldwide. For retailers or studios that choose to remain open for business overnight, the Wake is an exciting way to engage customers, promote your business, and collect donations to help underprivileged youth around the world!

Resources: Pledge Form, Poster, Suggested Promotions/Activities

Lead

Community Groups

What better way to develop a greater sense of community than to come together for one night and contribute to the global fight against HIV/AIDS? The 24 Hour Wake is a remarkable way for neighbours to get to know each other, learn about HIV, and inspire community action! All proceeds raised from the event will assist underprivileged youth around the world.

Resources: Pledge Form, Poster

Other

This is not your average sleepover. Instead, consider the 24 Hour Wake the most important slumber party of the year! Or, the slumber party your parents WANT you to attend. Spend quality time with your friends while helping to raise money to support HIV-affected youth around the world. Your overnight adventures will also help you gain a better sense of the struggles and mental tenacity of people affected by HIV.

Resources: Pledge Forms, suggested activities (including recommended movies, books, games)
